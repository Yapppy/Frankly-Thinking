About Me

I am a learner, thinker, and writer.


Hello, I'm Yapppy.

As of 2022, I am an university student studying in Malaysia. My plan is to mass communication studies for my degrees. Public relations and marketing pathways, in particular.

If I have to describe myself, I would say that I am a normal person with no outstanding talents.

Having just reached legal age, I felt that I am being left behind by my peers. My hobbies are just leisure entertainments for me. Unlike others, I don't compete nor I train. I neither do sports or arts. I just consume.

However, I wish to improve. I wish to create a version of me that has great values.

In this hope, I tried and suceed at being consious of my actions and thoughts. Nontheless, I wish to find others that think alike, and to discuss together. So, the idea for a blog emerged and was materialized.
